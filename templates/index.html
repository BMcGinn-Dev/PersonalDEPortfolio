Algorithmic NFL Spread Selector

Named as the "RoachFinder", this live web app scrapes data from various sources to feed an algorithm of my own creation to output a NFL game "spread" which is placed on major sportsbooks. Last year, it reported a season-long accuracy rating of 55.098% which, even when accounting for sportsbooks standard rake of -110 odds, correlates to a $2390.89 profit at season end.
